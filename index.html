Our Services

At Pramie Power, we specialize in crafting high-quality, innovative solar energy solutions. Our expertise lies in designing and delivering solar technologies that enhance both energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.

On Grid

An on-grid solar system connects to the public electricity grid, generating power through solar panels and feeding it into the grid. It reduces electricity bills via net metering and requires no batteries, making it cost-effective. However, it doesn’t provide backup power during outages, relying entirely on the grid’s availability.

Hybrid

A hybrid solar system combines both on-grid and off-grid features. It uses solar panels, a battery bank, and is connected to the electricity grid. This setup allows you to store excess energy in batteries for use during power outages while also exporting surplus power to the grid, offering flexibility and reliability.

Power/Lift Backup

A power or lift backup solar system is designed to provide uninterrupted electricity to critical systems like elevators, lifts, or essential appliances during power outages. It combines solar panels with battery storage and sometimes a grid connection, ensuring continuous operation, improved safety, and energy savings in residential or commercial buildings.

Frequently-Asked Questions

+

Do solar panels work during a power outage?

Standard on-grid systems do not work during power outages for safety reasons. However, hybrid or off-grid systems with battery storage can continue to supply electricity during blackouts, ensuring uninterrupted power.

+

What is a solar power system and how does it work?

A solar power system uses solar panels to convert sunlight into electricity. This electricity can be used immediately, stored in batteries, or sent to the grid depending on the system type (on-grid, off-grid, or hybrid).

+

What are the different types of solar systems?

The main types are: On-grid (connected to the utility grid), Off-grid (independent with battery storage), Hybrid (combines both with batteries and grid support).

+

Will solar panels work during cloudy days or at night?

Solar panels generate less electricity on cloudy days and do not work at night. However,hybrid or off-grid systems with batteries can store excess power for later use.

+

Can I completely eliminate my electricity bill with solar?

With a properly sized solar system, especially off-grid or hybrid, you can significantly reduce or eliminate your electricity bill. On-grid systems offer savings through net metering.

+

How much maintenance do solar panels require?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Cleaning them a few times a year and occasional system checks ensure optimal performance.

+

How long do solar panels last?

Net metering allows you to export excess electricity to the grid and earn credits, which offset your electricity consumption from the grid.

+

Is solar energy suitable for my home or business?

If your location receives adequate sunlight and has roof space or land for panels, solar is suitable. A site assessment can help determine feasibility and cost savings.

+

What is the cost of installing a solar system?

The cost depends on the type and size of the system, your energy needs, and local subsidies. Though the initial investment is higher, solar pays back through energy savings over time.
